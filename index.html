My name’s Michael Keane, but my friends call me Keane (it’s way more original than the most popular baby name from 64-98, a very impressive 35 year run, by the way). I just graduated from the University of Rochester, which was fun, and now I’m unemployed, which is less fun. I specialize in branding and visual design, particularly across digital mediums.

I grew up on the internet, and have paid close attention to how it’s grown and changed in the past 18 years. I picked up graphic design on online forums around middle school, and I’ve sat nicely in the intersection of design and digital ever since (except for a brief stint in pre-med).

That’s about it.

I listen to hip-hop (favorite artists are A Tribe Called Quest, GoldLink, and Kendrick), I love making guacamole, and my favorite movie is BladeRunner 2049. The original is not nearly as good. Call me on that.
